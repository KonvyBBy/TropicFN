Account Security Guide

Follow this recovery request process to keep your account secure.

Important Security Notice

The goal is NOT to change the email — this is simply a periodic security check.

Steps

  1. 1

    ✅ Update Email Address

    Go to:

    https://www.epicgames.com/help/wizards/w6?s=0

    Select Update Email Address

    Use a new email you create yourself.

  2. 2

    ✅ Find Account

    Choose Find account using email address

    Enter the most recently used email connected to the account you purchased.

  3. 3

    ✅ Email Change Question

    When asked: Have you ever changed your account email address?

    Select No or Not Sure

    After entering the code, choose No One / Not Sure again if prompted.

  4. 4

    ✅ Name & Location

    Enter basic info such as:

    • Name: John Jones (or any name)
    • Location: Your current location
  5. 5

    ✅ Connected Accounts

    Select No or Not Sure

  6. 6

    ✅ Payment Information

    Select No or Not Sure

  7. 7

    ✅ Reason & Submit

    Choose: I lost access to my email

    Then submit the request.

Important

  • 1 Do NOT try to force an email change.
  • 2 Simply submit a recovery request.
  • 3 Submit a new request every time they email you back that it has been denied.
  • 4 Record the date each time you submit it.

This helps maintain account security over time.

Change Your Rambler Email Password

If your account uses a Rambler.ru email, you should also update that email password for added security.

  1. 1 Go to mail.rambler.ru
  2. 2 Log in with your Rambler email and current password
  3. 3 Navigate to Settings / Account Settings
  4. 4 Select Change Password and set a strong new password
Tip: Use a unique password you do not use anywhere else.
